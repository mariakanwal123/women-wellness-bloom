Frequently Asked Questions
Common questions & troubleshooting
Q
Does Lumina save my data permanently?
Lumina runs entirely in your browser. Data persists while your session is active. For permanent storage, use the Export CSV button in the Hormones tab to download your hormone history as a file you can save and re-import or share with a doctor.
Q
The hormone sliders ‚Äî are they based on real lab values?
No. The sliders (1-10) reflect your subjective perception of how each hormone feels based on your symptoms. For actual lab value interpretation, use the Hormone Calculator tab ‚Üí Enter Lab Values mode, where you input real blood test numbers.
Q
How do I use this app for my morning routine?
Recommended daily flow: 1) Open Home ‚Üí read daily tip. 2) Supplements ‚Üí tap taken pills for AM doses. 3) Hormones ‚Üí drag sliders + save. 4) Stress ‚Üí set morning stress level. 5) Goals ‚Üí update any counters. Total time: under 3 minutes.
Q
Can I share data with my doctor?
Yes ‚Äî use the Export CSV button in the Hormones tab to download a spreadsheet of your full hormone history (date, time, cycle day, mood, all 4 hormone scores, and symptoms). This is a clean data file you can email to your doctor or import into any spreadsheet app.
Q
What's the difference between the Hormone Tracker and Hormone Calculator?
Hormone Tracker (Hormones tab) = daily symptom diary. You rate how you feel, save history, and build a log over time. Hormone Calculator (H. Calculator tab) = analysis tool. Either run the 15-question symptom quiz to get a score-based breakdown, or enter your actual blood test lab values for a detailed clinical interpretation.
